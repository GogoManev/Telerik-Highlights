8.2.2025:

Made this project ;) Every game now has a "Go Back" and "Restart" buttons under (or above) the game canvas
Hit the Ball: Fixed collisions between the mouse and the ball and increased the difficulty. Every 5 points, the ball becomes faster
Racer: Swapped controls (WASD for Player 1, Arrows for Player 2). Decreased the enemy helicopters form 100 to 50.
Breakout: Fixed a bug where balls won't spawn on the first click and where the maximum balls weren't 10.
cock cliccer: Fixed several bugs: When your points would not match the points you had prior to refreshing the page, when the updates
weren't registered after refreshing, where autoclick began automatically and would not register when the game has been refreshed.
The autoclick upgrade now gives points at a slower rate. Increased the points requirement for future additional points upgrades.
Using upgrades now charges the amount of points you needed. Removed the unused shop section. Changed some text strings.
